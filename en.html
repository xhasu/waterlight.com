What is
WaterLight?

A device that provides electrical power using only salt water.

This thanks to the ionization of an electrolyte made up of salt water that transforms the magnesium on the inside into electrical energy.

Clean and renewable energy

For up to 45 days with just half a liter of salt water.

Whenever and wherever

lighting available 24/7 in any location.

Rechargeable battery

for mobile and electronic devices.

Easily adaptable

to any canoe to improve the fishing process.

Why
Waterlight?

THE WORLD NEEDS A CLEAN ENERGY REVOLUTION BECAUSE

About 840 million people have no access to electricity.
According to the United Nations, electricity demand is expected to increase by 70% by 2035.
It's estimated that fossil fuel reserves will be depleted in the next 52 years.
The solution

Waterlight produces electricity from a renewable natural resource as salt water.

This improves the quality of life of communities without access to this vital resource, offering an energy alternative with the power to supply a large demand while caring for the environment.
